## What's Included

- Governance policies and enforcement mechanisms
- Architecture Decision Records (governance-focused)
- AI agent governance protocols and trust boundaries
- Metadata schemas and validation tooling
- CI/CD governance enforcement workflows
- Onboarding and contribution guides

## What's Excluded

Infrastructure-specific details (cloud accounts, domains, cluster
configurations, Terraform modules, Helm charts) have been removed or
replaced with placeholders like `[AWS_ACCOUNT_ID]` and `[PRIMARY_DOMAIN]`.
